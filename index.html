Our Recent Trip to Karnataka

Trip Overview

The Department of BBA Tourism organized an exciting trip to Karnataka, visiting some of the state's most famous attractions. Our faculty staff included:

  • Dr. Sangeeta Peters - Head of Department
  • Mr. Marvel - Assistant Professor

All students from the department participated in this enriching experience.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is an architectural masterpiece showcasing a blend of Hindu, Muslim, Rajput, and Gothic styles. It is one of the top-rated tourist destinations in India.

Udupi

Udupi is renowned for its temples, particularly the Sri Krishna Temple. The town is a hub of spirituality and offers a glimpse into Karnataka's rich cultural heritage.

Chikmagalur

Chikmagalur is famous for its lush coffee plantations and stunning hill stations. The scenic beauty and pleasant climate make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.

Conclusion

This trip provided a wonderful opportunity for students to explore the cultural and historical richness of Karnataka. We look forward to more such educational excursions in the future!
